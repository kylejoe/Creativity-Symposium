Attend

Join us Saturday, June 7, 2014 from 10:00am - 3:00pm at the Fremont Abbey Arts Center. Tickets are $100 and include heavy horderves for lunch, water/tea, a live performance of Jen Hopper, The Bravest Women in Seattle, keynote speaker Patti Dubrowolski from Up Your Creative Genius, and several other speakers through 3 breakout sessions, along with quality connections and good company. The event is created as a whole "experience" in three segments, so please plan on attending the entire duration. For the latest updates, visit our Facebook page.

Program

  • 9:30 Doors open to public, Registration and Mingle
  • 10:00 Event begins: Introduction
  • 10:05 Musical Guest Performance
  • 10:15 Breakout Session 1: At Home
  • 11:00 Break
  • 11:15 Breakout Session 1: In Community
  • 12:00 Lunch, Cooking “Chef-Off” with local chefs
  • 1:00 Mastermind Circles
  • 1:30 Session 3: In the World
  • 2:15 Keynote Speaker: Patti Dobrowolski from Up Your Creative Genius
  • 2:50 Closing Remarks
  • 3:00: Event completes: mingle and connecting
  • *tentative and could be subject to change

Location

ADDRESS 4272 Fremont Avenue North, Seattle, WA

PARKING There is ample street parking around the Fremont Abbey.
