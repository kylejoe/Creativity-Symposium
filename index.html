Creativity Symposium for Entrepreneurial Women

The Creativity Symposium for Puget Sound Entrepreneurial Women was created to unleash joy, creativity and community for entrepreneurial women. The seminar is a local, independently-organized event designed to provide relevant, credible, highly useful and worthwhile resources to expand opportunities and connections through speakers, workshops, mastermind circles, and networking.

Attendees will have the opportunity to share their message, network, learn, and be inspired by tapping into the greater collective wisdom of community, engaging the whole woman, not just the left or right brain, but integrating all the parts of the roles played in life. Ultimately, we aim to transform the future of global leadership through providing a forum to create high level connections, support, mentorship and resources for women entrepreneurs.
